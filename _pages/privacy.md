This site is a personal blog. Here is what it collects, and why.

## Analytics

I use **Google Analytics 4** (measurement ID `G-6VYW7NDKTY`) to count how many people read the site and which posts they read. GA4 drops cookies in your browser and sends your IP, browser, and the page you viewed to Google. I have IP anonymization enabled. I do not link any of this to personally identifying information on my end.

You can opt out at any time by installing the [Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or by using a browser that blocks trackers.

See [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for the full details.

## Comments

Comments are powered by [**Giscus**](https://giscus.app), which stores them as discussions on the GitHub repository for this site. To comment, you sign in with your GitHub account and GitHub stores your identity and comment history under their terms.

If you never comment, no data flows to GitHub from here.

See [GitHub's privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

## Newsletter

If you sign up for the email newsletter, your email address is stored by [**Buttondown**](https://buttondown.com) on my behalf. I use it only to send newsletter issues. Unsubscribe links are in every email.

See [Buttondown's privacy policy](https://buttondown.com/legal/privacy).

## Contact form

The contact form is handled by [**Formspree**](https://formspree.io). When you submit, your name, email, and message are sent through Formspree to my inbox.

See [Formspree's privacy policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy).

## Cookies

The site itself does not set cookies. Cookies are only set by the third parties listed above (primarily GA4 and any GitHub session cookies on comment threads).
